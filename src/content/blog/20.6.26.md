This image might be one of my oldest. Obviously while getting this website set up, my old images I will have to cycle through first.

Needless to say, this is Jupiter. I took this on what I assume very turbulent weather. Captured with the Celestron NexImage 10, a fairly competent camera for it's price. Funny story though, I couldn't seem to get it to work as advertised, not then, not now. As you can see by the image, it's very grainy, out of focus, and blurry. No matter how much i fiddled with the settings, it just wouldn't give me that quality. 

It is probably some fault of mine rather than the camera, but you never know. I've tried to fix it, even in later images of other planets, the result is the same.

I'll have to troubleshoot it more, but as a sort of foreshadowing, it won't matter much. 

Anyway, enjoy the image, it's pretty special to me, representing a fresh start in this hobby.
