This was quite long ago, about 2 years ago when I first got my 8" Schmidt Cassegrain and a T-Ring adapter for my D3200. Don't remember much, but this is probably about 50 images stacked and processed on PIPP and Registax, and saturation boosted on Photoshop.

You can clearly see deposits of metals, Titanium, sulfur, etc., Hence the color.

Funny story; my sort of naive side thought that this would be good enough for an APOD submission...You probably know the outcome of that judgement.

From this point on, it only went uphill.
