Possibly the most beautiful images I've taken of the night sky till date; and i've got a good story for them.

It was late in the night, we stopped by somewhere dark with our RV in New Zealand. I set an alarm for about 4AM, because that's when the forecast said it would be clear. And what do you know, it was. 

Now, after I was done being awe-struck by it and hurting my neck looking up to it, I realised, that I didn't have any equipment that was tuned to photograph this. I had my telescope, but that's a no for wide field, and I did have my D3200, it's just that it was a bit old. 

So I settled on my GoPro on night mode with a ~30 sec exposure. And for what it is, it turned out pretty great.

To the naked eye, it was more or less black and white, which is to be expected, nevertheless, stunning. 

When I returned with the images, the processing wasn't much, just boosting saturation and sharpening/lightening a bit to really make the colors pop. So these images are a bit 'fake', but only to humans.

Anyway, enjoy, and feel free to contact me or even download these images as a wallpaper or something as I've finally added the download feature.
