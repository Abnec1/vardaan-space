Most of my posts are plain Markdown, but I keep MDX enabled for entries where I want richer structure (inline callouts, custom components, and quick visual comparisons).

## My rule of thumb

- Use Markdown for standard observing logs.
- Use MDX only when a post needs custom presentation.

This keeps writing friction low but still gives flexibility when a story needs more than text and a single image.

## Typical MDX use cases here

- Before/after processing comparisons.
- Highlight boxes for equipment experiments.
- Inline reusable observing checklist snippets.
