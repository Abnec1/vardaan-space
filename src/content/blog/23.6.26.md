Again, just clearing out the stock here; but this is my first-ever *proper* image of Saturn, I've taken other ones, but they were through my DSLR.

This image was processed through PIPP, stacked on AutoStakkert, and sharpened in Registax. I'd say the result turned out pretty good inspite of bad data/seeing conditions; Dubai isn't exactly the best place for astrophotography.

You can clearly see the ring structure around the planet, and the overall shape of the planet, no surface detail, yet, for this image.

As I will talk about later in this blog, I hope to upgrade my camera, from the NexImage, which to be honest hasn't been all that great for me. As for what I will choose, well i'll explain that in a later blog post.

For now, enjoy.
